Who We Are

Healing the Whole Person

The Get Back Foundation was born from a belief that every person — no matter where they've been — has the capacity to heal, grow, and fully return to life. We serve individuals in recovery from addiction with a model that doesn't just address sobriety, but restores every part of what addiction takes away.

We understand that lasting recovery requires more than willpower. It demands community, professional support, family, and purpose. That's why we've built a four-pillar model that addresses the whole human being.

See Our Programs

What We Do

Four Pillars of Renewal

Our holistic approach addresses every dimension of recovery — because true healing leaves nothing behind.

🧠

Mental Health Support

Licensed counselors and peer support specialists provide one-on-one therapy, group sessions, and trauma-informed care to help participants heal from within. We treat the mind as the foundation of everything else.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Reunification

Addiction fractures families. Our family support program guides both participants and their loved ones through healing, communication, and rebuilding trust — because no one fully recovers alone.

💼

Job Training & Career Readiness

From resume building and interview coaching to vocational training and employer partnerships, we open doors to meaningful employment — giving participants the dignity of work and financial independence.

🌿

Holistic Wellness

Nutrition, physical health, mindfulness, and community connection form the bedrock of whole-person care. We help participants build sustainable routines that support lifelong well-being.

Our Mission Statement

"Through a whole-person healing journey that nurtures mental health, rebuilds family bonds, and unlocks career potential, we walk alongside those reclaiming their lives — empowering them to rediscover their strength, their dignity, and their rightful place in the world."
